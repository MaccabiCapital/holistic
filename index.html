What is Homeopathy?

Homeopathy is a system of natural health care that has been in worldwide use for over 200 years. Homeopathy treats each person as a unique individual with the aim of stimulating their own healing ability. A homeopath selects the most appropriate medicine based on the individual's specific symptoms and personal level of health. It is recognized by the World Health Organization as the second largest therapeutic system in use in the world.

Principles

  • The individual is seen as a whole person, and both the physical and psychological dimensions are taken into account
  • The environment an individual lives in is recognized as being inextricably linked to his or her health
  • The first rule of therapeutic care is to do no harm and protect the body from unnecessary distress
  • Homeopathic medicines support and stimulate the body's natural repair and defense mechanisms

Etymology

"Homeopathy" combines two Greek words: homoios ("similar") and pathos ("illness"). Together, they describe what homeopathic medicines do, i.e., treat using "what is similar to the illness."

Natural Medicine?

Homeopathy uses natural substances derived from plant, mineral and animal sources. But homeopathy is much more than natural medicine—it is a therapeutic method of healing founded on the treatment of similar with similar.

What Kind of Conditions Can Homeopathy Treat?

Homeopathy is a therapeutic method used to treat a wide range of common ailments such as the flu, colds, migraines, wounds, sprains, insomnia, stress, and dental problems. It is also efficient in the treatment of more chronic conditions.

Homeopathy is a Natural Healing and Natural Medicine

There are generally no contraindications between traditional and homeopathic medicines when taken simultaneously. Although homeopathy is effective on its own in treating a variety of conditions, your health care provider may suggest complementing treatment with traditional medicine.

Can Homeopathic Medicines be Used to Treat Chronic Conditions?

Yes. When dealing with a chronic condition, such as allergies or herpes, homeopathic treatment aims to stop its recurrence. And even with conditions such as cancer, it can be used to accompany a traditional treatment.

Do Homeopathic Medicines Act Quickly?

Yes. When used for acute, occasional illnesses, homeopathic medicines can take effect very quickly, sometimes in minutes.

Who Benefits from Homeopathy?

Homeopathy is an ideal form of treatment for the whole family – from infants to the elderly, and everyone in-between. Its natural medicine is extremely effective in treating common conditions (coughs, colds, flu-like symptoms, teething, muscle pain, etc.), as well as chronic ones (asthma, migraine, anxiety, fatigue, depression, arthritis, etc.).

How to Take Homeopathic Medicine?

  • Avoid Touching the Medicine. Make sure that your hands are free of strong odors. Shake directly into the cap of the bottle and then into the mouth.
  • The Mouth Should be Substance Free. When taking the medicine, the mouth should be free from food, liquids (other than water), tobacco, toothpaste, mouthwash, strong breath mints, or anything which could interfere with absorption.
  • Protect the Medicine. Direct sunlight, heat over 110 degrees (such as a car in summer) and strong odors can damage homeopathic medicines.
  • Discard Spilled or Transferred Medicine. If some of the medicine should spill from its original vial to another surface, please throw away the spilled medicine.

Why Do I Have to Let the Pellets Dissolve Under My Tongue?

When Natural Medicine is taken sublingually, the ingredients in the pellets go directly to your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system.

Ways Homeopathic Medicines May be Anti-doted

The everyday items on this list are antidotes that will cancel the effects of your Homeopathic Natural Medicine:

  • Coffee: Coffee frequently antidotes Homeopathic medicines, occasionally after only one cup.
  • Camphor: Skin or mucous membrane application of camphor may antidote medicines.
  • Anything to Which You Are Particularly Sensitive: Avoid any food, chemical, or substance which you know causes severe reactions.
  • Electric Blankets: Waterbed warmers and heating pads are OK and will not cause a problem.
  • Routine Dental Work: Cleaning of the teeth is OK. However the actual drilling, and perhaps the Novocain, will antidote.
  • Skin Medicines: It is not uncommon for Homeopathic medicines to bring back old skin symptoms. Please do not use lotions, creams or salves on these areas.
  • Tea Tree Oil: Tea tree oil will antidote.
  • Diagnostic Tests: Blood work will not antidote. Routine X rays are also not a problem. Mammograms or ultrasounds can sometimes antidote.
